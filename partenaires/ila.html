Qui sont-ils

Who they are

L'International Lawyers Alliance (ILA) fournit le cadre juridique cooperatif de KONKRET. Structure REI-inspiree. Subchapter T pour les dividendes de patronage. Protection de la propriete cooperative.

The International Lawyers Alliance (ILA) provides KONKRET's cooperative legal framework. REI-inspired structure. Subchapter T for patronage dividends. Cooperative ownership protection.

Leur role dans KONKRET

Their role in KONKRET

Drafting cooperative bylaws. Micro-equity structure. Anti-extraction protection (no external investor can acquire more than 5%). US/Haiti tax compliance.
