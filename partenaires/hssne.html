Haiti Solidarity of the Northeast ... diasporic Catholic parish coalition

Qui ils sont

HSSNE (Haiti Solidarity of the Northeast) est une coalition de paroisses catholiques basee a Jersey City, New Jersey. La paroisse d'ancrage est St. Patrick Jersey City. Le reseau s'etend sur plus de 60 paroisses de l'Archidiocese de Newark et touche environ 26,000 personnes nees en Haiti dans le corridor diasporique du Nord-Est americain.

Leur role dans Konkret

Souscripteur du Mizak Mega Hub. HSSNE finance le sikisal de $3,600 qui lance le Cohort Fondateur Mizak. Au-dela du financement initial, HSSNE est le pont diasporique qui connecte les jeunes haitiens du sud d'Haiti aux ressources et au reseau de la diaspora du Nord-Est. Si le pilote Mizak atteint ses objectifs, HSSNE peut souscrire a la mise a niveau Hub ($55,000, 15 jeunes) en Annee 2.

Statut

Partenariat actif. Mizak Mega Hub en Etape 1 sur 7 (intake form + nomination Manadje Asosye). Lancement du Cohort Fondateur prevu en 2026.

Who they are

HSSNE (Haiti Solidarity of the Northeast) is a Catholic parish coalition based in Jersey City, New Jersey. The anchor parish is St. Patrick Jersey City. The network extends across more than 60 parishes in the Archdiocese of Newark and reaches approximately 26,000 Haiti-born people in the Northeast diaspora corridor.

Their role in Konkret

Subscriber of the Mizak Mega Hub. HSSNE funds the $3,600 sikisal that launches the Mizak Founding Cohort. Beyond the initial funding, HSSNE is the diasporic bridge connecting Haitian youth in southern Haiti to resources and to the Northeast diaspora network. If the Mizak pilot hits its marks, HSSNE can subscribe for Hub upgrade ($55,000, 15 youth) in Year 2.

Status

Active partnership. Mizak Mega Hub in Step 1 of 7 (intake form + Manager Associate nomination). Founding Cohort launch planned 2026.
