Ecological sanitation and compost

Qui sont-ils

Who they are

SOIL (Sustainable Organic Integrated Livelihoods) est le leader mondial de l'assainissement ecologique en Haiti. Toilettes seches. Compostage thermophile. 10+ ans d'operations.

SOIL is the global leader in ecological sanitation in Haiti. Dry toilets. Thermophilic composting. 10+ years of operations.

Leur role dans KONKRET

Their role in KONKRET

Deploiement de toilettes Konpos Lakay sur les sites KONKRET. Fourniture de compost certifie pour les lits sureleves. Donnees comparatives rendement avec/sans compost.

Deployment of Konpos Lakay toilets at KONKRET sites. Supply of certified compost for raised beds. Comparative yield data with/without compost.

Ce que KONKRET leur apporte

What KONKRET brings them

25 sites d'installation. Donnees d'utilisation en temps reel via NFC. Canal de financement (abonnes financent les toilettes). Preuve d'impact quantifiee.

25 installation sites. Real-time usage data via NFC. Funding channel (subscribers fund the toilets). Quantified impact proof.
