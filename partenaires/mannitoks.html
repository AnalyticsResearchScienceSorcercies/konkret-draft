Qui sont-ils

Who they are

Mannitoks est une entreprise technologique specialisee dans les attestations blockchain, les systemes NFC, et la verification d'identite. Basee en Haiti.

Mannitoks is a technology company specializing in blockchain attestations, NFC systems, and identity verification. Based in Haiti.

Trois niveaux de verification

Three levels of verification

Niveau 1 — Financier (US-side, on-chain)

Level 1 — Financial (US-side, on-chain)

Coinbase Commerce → USDC → Safe Multisig (3/5) → 0xSplits. Chaque transaction visible sur BaseScan.

Coinbase Commerce → USDC → Safe Multisig (3/5) → 0xSplits. Every transaction visible on BaseScan.

Niveau 2 — Operationnel (Haiti-side, attestations)

Level 2 — Operational (Haiti-side, attestations)

Mannitoks enregistre les attestations terrain: inscriptions de jeunes, livraisons d'equipement, paiements de salaires, scans NFC. Horodate. Infalsifiable. Lie au Niveau 1.

Mannitoks records ground attestations: youth enrollments, equipment deliveries, salary payments, NFC scans. Timestamped. Tamper-proof. Linked to Level 1.

Niveau 3 — Gouvernance (cooperative, micro-equite)

Level 3 — Governance (cooperative, micro-equity)

Chaque trimestre travaille = parts cooperatives accumulees. Droits de vote proportionnels. Registre sur la chaine. Les jeunes sont proprietaires, pas employes.

Each quarter worked = cooperative shares accumulated. Proportional voting rights. Register on-chain. Youth are owners, not employees.
