3 niveaux de blockchain

3 blockchain levels

Niveau 1 — Financier (Base L2)

Level 1 — Financial (Base L2)

Smart contracts sur Base (Ethereum L2). USDC stablecoin. Safe Multisig 3/5. 0xSplits pour la repartition automatique. Gas fees < $0.01.

Smart contracts on Base (Ethereum L2). USDC stablecoin. Safe Multisig 3/5. 0xSplits for automatic distribution. Gas fees < $0.01.

Niveau 2 — Operationnel (Mannitoks)

Level 2 — Operational (Mannitoks)

Attestations EAS. Chaque inscription, paiement, livraison, scan est un enregistrement permanent. Horodate. Infalsifiable.

EAS attestations. Every enrollment, payment, delivery, scan is a permanent record. Timestamped. Tamper-proof.

Niveau 3 — Gouvernance (Cooperative)

Level 3 — Governance (Cooperative)

Registre de micro-equite on-chain. Parts cooperatives = tokens non-transferables (soulbound). Droits de vote proportionnels aux trimestres travailles.

On-chain micro-equity register. Cooperative shares = non-transferable tokens (soulbound). Voting rights proportional to quarters worked.
