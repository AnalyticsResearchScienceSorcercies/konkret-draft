Ce que fait KONKRET

What KONKRET does

SOIL Konpos Lakay

Toilettes seches a compostage. Les dechets humains deviennent du compost certifie apres traitement thermophile. Utilise dans nos lits sureleves. Circuit ferme.

Dry composting toilets. Human waste becomes certified compost after thermophilic treatment. Used in our raised beds. Closed loop.

Gestion des dechets (FatraKa)

Waste management (FatraKa)

Collecte et tri des dechets solides. Les plastiques sont separes. Les organiques rejoignent le compost. Les jeunes gerent la collecte.

Solid waste collection and sorting. Plastics separated. Organics join the compost. Youth manage the collection.

Eau propre

Clean water

Filtres ceramiques + chloration solaire. Chaque site a un point d'eau propre accessible a la communaute.

Ceramic filters + solar chlorination. Each site has a clean water point accessible to the community.
