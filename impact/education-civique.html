Une carte de bibliotheque est le premier acte civique.

A library card is the first civic act.

Le probleme

The problem

Haiti a 2 bibliotheques publiques fonctionnelles pour 12 millions de personnes. L'education financiere est absente du curriculum. Les jeunes n'ont aucun mecanisme de participation civique.

Haiti has 2 functioning public libraries for 12 million people. Financial literacy is absent from the curriculum. Youth have no mechanism for civic participation.

Ce que fait KONKRET

What KONKRET does

Bibliotheque communautaire

Community Library

Chaque site a une bibliotheque de 50+ livres. La carte 5-en-1 sert de carte de bibliotheque. Emprunts suivis. Lecture recompensee.

Each site has a library of 50+ books. The 5-in-1 card serves as a library card. Borrowing tracked. Reading rewarded.

Litteratie financiere

Financial Literacy

Les jeunes apprennent a lire leurs propres transactions blockchain. Ils comprennent les splits, les pourcentages, les taux de change. L'argent n'est plus abstrait.

Youth learn to read their own blockchain transactions. They understand splits, percentages, exchange rates. Money is no longer abstract.

Integration marchands

Merchant Onboarding

Les jeunes forment les marchands locaux a accepter les cartes NFC. Ils deviennent l'infrastructure civique de leur communaute.

Youth train local merchants to accept NFC cards. They become the civic infrastructure of their community.
