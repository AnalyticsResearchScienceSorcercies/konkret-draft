Phi (Φ) = 0.987... le coefficient de recuperation

Phi (Φ) = 0.987... the recovery coefficient

Sur chaque dollar envoye en "aide" a Haiti, $0.987 est recupere par des entites non-haitiennes avant de toucher le sol haitien. Phi mesure combien le systeme reprend avant livraison. Pour Haiti: presque tout.

For every dollar sent in "aid" to Haiti, $0.987 is recovered by non-Haitian entities before it touches Haitian soil. Phi measures how much the system takes back before delivery. For Haiti: almost everything.

Etude de cas: Chemonics International

Case study: Chemonics International

Un seul contrat: $259 millions. Base a Washington, DC. Personnel international, bureaux internationaux, sous-traitants internationaux. One contract: $259 million. Based in Washington, DC. International staff, international offices, international subcontractors. No permanent infrastructure left in Haiti after the contract ends. This is the largest recipient of USAID aid in the world. Not the most effective. The largest.
