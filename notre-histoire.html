15 ans. Pas un rapport. Une histoire.

15 years. Not a report. A story.

2011

Dan arrive en Haiti. Apres le seisme de 2010. Travail communautaire. Premiers contacts avec les jeunes.

Dan arrives in Haiti. After the 2010 earthquake. Community work. First contact with youth.

2013

Premiers programmes agricoles. Jardins communautaires. Moringa. Apprentissage par essai et erreur.

First agricultural programs. Community gardens. Moringa. Learning by trial and error.

2016

Expansion. De 1 a 5 sites. Premiers partenariats (HELP, SOIL).

Expansion. From 1 to 5 sites. First partnerships (HELP, SOIL).

2019

Elevage et transformation. Lapins, cabris. Agripure rejoint le reseau. La Bwat Rekolt prend forme.

Livestock and processing. Rabbits, goats. Agripure joins the network. The Harvest Box takes shape.

2020

COVID et resilience. Les sites continuent pendant la pandemie. Preuve de resilience du modele decentralise.

COVID and resilience. Sites continue during the pandemic. Proof of resilience of the decentralized model.

2023

Rencontre Wesley. EEDTM rencontre KONKRET. Les mathematiques de l'extraction appliquees a la solution.

Wesley joins. EEDTM meets KONKRET. The mathematics of extraction applied to the solution.

2024

sites Cohort Fondateur. cohort Premye Yo en lancement. 2 departements actifs. Le modele franchise est prouve.

sites Cohort Fondateur. Premye Yo cohort in launch. 2 active departments. The franchise model is proven.

2025

Blockchain et cooperative. Coinbase Commerce. 0xSplits. Mannitoks. Cartes NFC. La transparence devient structurelle.

Blockchain and cooperative. Coinbase Commerce. 0xSplits. Mannitoks. NFC cards. Transparency becomes structural.

2026

konkret-haiti.com. La Bwat Rekolt en ligne. Abonnements ouverts. Le circuit est ferme.

konkret-haiti.com. The Harvest Box online. Subscriptions open. The circuit is closed.
