Le site

The site

Mizak est dans la commune de La Vallee-de-Jacmel, departement Sud-Est. Coordonnees fixees: 18.2592°N, −72.6878°W, altitude 535m. A 14 km de la ville de Jacmel et 7 km de Ridore.

Mizak is in the La Vallee-de-Jacmel commune, Sud-Est department. Coordinates pinned: 18.2592°N, −72.6878°W, elevation 535m. 14 km from Jacmel city, 7 km from Ridore.

Le sol

The soil

pH 6.0 sur les 30 premiers cm. Argile-limoneuse. Carbone organique du sol 45.9 g/kg en surface. Capacite d'echange cationique 22.9-27.5 cmol/kg. Zero remediation requise. Sol prime pour les cultures de rente et l'agroforesterie.

pH 6.0 throughout the top 30 cm. Clay-loam texture. Soil organic carbon 45.9 g/kg at surface. Cation exchange capacity 22.9-27.5 cmol/kg. Zero remediation needed. Prime ground for cash crops and agroforestry.

Strategie de cultures

Crop strategy

Strategie agroforestiere rurale (pas le modele urbain Cite Soleil): herbe a elephant en bandes de niveau pour le controle de pente et le revenu de transition; bambou en perimetre pour les credits carbone; canopee native (acajou, cedre espagnol, acacia natif); agroforesterie alimentaire (cafe, cacao, fruit a pain, mangue, agrumes, avocat); jardins de cuisine (moringa).

Rural agroforestry stack (not the urban Cite Soleil model): elephant grass in contour strips for slope control and bridge revenue; bamboo perimeter for carbon credits; native canopy (mahogany, Spanish cedar, native acacia); food agroforestry (coffee, cacao, breadfruit, mango, citrus, avocado); kitchen gardens (moringa).
